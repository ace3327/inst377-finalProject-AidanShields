About This Website

This site allows you to explore the Metropolitan Museum of Art's vast open-access collection using the official Met Collection API.

On the Search page, you can enter terms like artist names, time periods, mediums, and departments to filter your search results. Each search is also recorded anonymously and visualized using charts on the same page to help us understand popular interest areas.
